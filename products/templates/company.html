About VK Trading Company

From our humble beginnings to becoming a trusted name in hardware trading

Our Journey

From our humble beginnings in 2003, VK Trading Company was founded with a clear vision: To offer reliable, high-grade hardware solutions to businesses, contractors, and individual customers alike.

Over the years, we've grown steadily, driven by an unwavering commitment to quality, honesty, and customer satisfaction. Today, we are recognized not just for the wide range of products we supply but also for the deep trust we've built with our loyal clientele.

Located in the bustling heart of Chawri Bazar, Delhi—India's historic hardware and industrial market—we have built a legacy of delivering top-quality hardware products at the most competitive prices.

Unmatched Quality

We believe quality is non-negotiable. All our products go through stringent quality checks to ensure durability and performance.

Best Price Guarantee

By leveraging long-standing supplier relationships and bulk trading capabilities, we pass on cost benefits directly to our customers.

Customer-First Approach

We aim to build long-term relationships—not just transactions. Our team is always ready to assist with bulk orders or custom requirements.
